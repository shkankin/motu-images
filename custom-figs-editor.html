How this maps to the app
Figures here live in localStorage at motu-custom-figs on the same origin as the app. The MOTU Vault app reads them at sync time and merges them in after the AF411 figures from figures.json.

ID prefixing: every saved figure gets an ID starting with custom-. When AF411 eventually adds the same figure with their own numeric ID, your custom entry stays separate. You can then either delete your custom entry (and re-tag your collection data on the official one) or leave both.

AF411 button on a custom figure: falls back to AF411's all-figures index page (since there's no deep-link target).

Line dropdown: pick the line the figure belongs to (Origins, Masterverse, etc.) — the app sorts it into the appropriate line's list automatically. Use group to nest it into a subline ("Deluxe", "Action Figures", "Vehicles & Playsets", etc.) — type the group exactly as AF411 uses it (case-sensitive) so the figure shows in the right subline tab.
